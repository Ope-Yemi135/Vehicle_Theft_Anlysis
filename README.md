#  Vehicle Theft Analytics (2022)

**Data-Driven Crime Pattern Analysis & Risk Profiling**

---

##  Project Overview

This project presents an exploratory and descriptive analytics assessment of national vehicle theft data for 2022. The objective was to uncover temporal patterns, geographic concentration, vehicle vulnerability factors, and risk clusters to support strategic security planning and operational decision-making.

The analysis leverages percentage distribution modeling, categorical segmentation, and trend evaluation to transform raw incident data into actionable intelligence.

---

##  Business Problem

Vehicle theft incidents are unevenly distributed across:

- Time periods  
- Geographic regions  
- Vehicle categories  
- Model years  

Stakeholders require structured analytical insights to:

- Identify peak theft periods  
- Detect high-risk geographic clusters  
- Determine the most vulnerable vehicle categories  
- Support data-driven enforcement and prevention strategies
